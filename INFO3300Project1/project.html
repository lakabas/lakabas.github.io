The Evolution of NBA Shooting

By Lev Akabas, Samin Foysol, Carrie Huang

Basketball fans often comment that the style of the game has been changing over the years. In the modern era, teams such as Stephen Curry's Golden State Warriors have won championships by taking three point shots at historic rates. Has evolution in shot selection led to more efficient shooting league-wide? Are NBA teams taking "smarter" shots than they used to? Using data from stats.nba.com, we analyzed the frequency of shots taken at different locations on the court and the efficiency of those shot attempts during select seasons over the past two decades: the 1997-1998 season, the 2006-2007 season, and the 2016-2017 seasons.
