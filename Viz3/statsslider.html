* Statistical Profile encompasses traditional box score stats such as points, shooting percentage, assists, rebounds, blocks, and steals
* Advanced Box Score Metrics includes VORP (Value Over Replacement Player), BPM (Box Plus-Minus), and WS (Win Shares), which are catch-all statistics generated using traditional box score stats
* Plus Minus / Impact Metrics includes RPM (Real Plus-Minus) and PIPM (Player Impact Plus-Minus), both of which attempt to estimate a player's impact on their team using plus-minus data
* Usage Rate is an estimate of the percentage of the team's offense possessions that a player uses
* Clutch Scoring encompasses both scoring volume and efficiency in the last five minutes of games when the score is within five points
* Team Performance includes team record and the team's net rating with a given player on the court
